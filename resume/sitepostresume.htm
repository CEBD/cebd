This page will help you create your online resume. Please note:

  • That the text of your resumé has to be completed and stored on your computer (or disk) in one of the four file types listed at the bottom in red.
  • That the resumé you submit should be a professional resumé that is viewable by employers. The resumé should not be the type of resumé for a keyword search. If you want help creating a professional resumé, please contact Student Development Services at Southwestern Oklahoma State University: (580) 774-3233.

The average time to complete this form and post you resumé is 15 minutes, so please ensure that your schedule will allow this much time before beginning. Please note that fields marked with an asterisk (*) must be be completed before the resumé can be posted. Also, resumés are posted for one year after the last updated date.

To select more than one criteria, click on the criteria and hold down the "control" key if you are using a Windows based PC or hold down the "command" (a.k.a. apple key) if you are using a Macintosh computer.
