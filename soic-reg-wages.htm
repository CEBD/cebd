SOIC Regional Economic Indicators:
Total Wages

he SOIC Regional Economic Indicators: Total Wages report provides the total amount of wages in the SOIC region, broken down by the two digit NAICS code.

The total wage figures include all wages and salaries paid to federal and covered employees including bonuses, commissions and cash value of all payment received in any medium other than cash. These wages include both taxable and non-taxable wages.

Counties included in the SOIC region are Beckham, Blaine, Caddo, Canadian, Comanche, Cotton, Custer, Dewey, Grady, Greer, Harmon, Jackson, Jefferson, Kingfisher, Kiowa, McClain, Roger Mills, Stephens, Tillman, and Washita.
