WOMA Meeting set for January 12th

  • January, 2004
  • by: Paul Walenciak

Freightliner Specialty Vehicles will host the Western Oklahoma Manufacturers' Association (WOMA) January meeting on January 12 in Clinton. Dinner will be at the Freightliner facility and will be hosted by two Western Oklahoma food processors. Granna's of Bessie will provide the entree and Wheatland Grains of Cordell will provide homemade bread. The meeting officially starts at 7:00 P.M. but everyone is invited to arrive byabout 6:30 to eat and network. Freightliner is located at 2300 South 13th Street, which is behind the Doane Pet Care Facility off Highway 183 justSouth of I-40.

Freightliner manufacturers towing vehicles used to haul cargo, pull boats on receiver hitch trailers, tow horse and utility trailers with ball hitches and large RV fifth wheel trailers. Their SportChassis is acclaimedas the best specialty tow vehicle in the industry.

I look forward to seeing you at the meeting. If you have any questions or need further information please contact Paul Walenciak at 580 774-7071.
