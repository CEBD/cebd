  • History: Raven Industries was founded in 1976 by Doyle and Fay Jackson and eventually turned over the leadership to Brian Jackson. They began doing contract packaging to fulfill a "niche" need for 3M Company. Since then the company has expanded its process capabilities to include Pad Printing and Screen Printing services. Raven has worked with many large corporations including 3M, Xerox, Verbatim, Roche, and Carlisle Food Service Products. Raven has based its success on quality and flexibility of service.
  • Products: Contract packaging services include: blister packaging, kit assembly, point-of-purchase display, clamshell packaging, unit cartoning, poly-bagging. Pad and Screen printing services offer close tolerance and multicolor on flat, cylindrical, irregular, and unconventional surfaces. Production capabilities range from low to high volume.
