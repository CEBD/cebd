  • History: Formed in 1985, Cyan Co., Inc., is located in the industrial park of Weatherford, OK. Its facility has a total space of approximately 4,000 square feet which is divided into offices, electronic testing, assembly, production, and prototyping. We keep thousands of electronic parts in stock for prototyping and repair. Some of the stock includes: resistors, capacitors, diodes, transistors, integrated circuits, relays, connectors, fuses inductors, transformers, standoffs, insulators, wire, ribbon cable, and screws. Cyan is known throughout Western Oklahoma for its electronic repair capabilities. We offer quick service at reasonable rates. The majority of our repair work involves printers, computers, monitors, and other computer-related equipment. In addition, we repair: industrial modules, temperature controllers, ultrasonic welders, programmable controllers, oil-field equipment, CNC equipment, avionics, power supplies, and more. The current count of items repaired is over 6,500.
  • Products: Our capabilities cover a wide range of services including: electronic/electromechanical engineering and design; software development; computer hardware development; product prototyping; electronic consulting services; and technical documentation. We have the capability to custom design and produce almost any type of electronic product. Some of the devices developed are: electronic circuits; prototype circuit boards; metallic enclosures; panels and brackets; custom connector and cable assemblies; aeronautic controls; solar controls; test equipment; power supplies; and battery chargers. Our specialties include: analog electronics; power control(including switch-mode power supplies and converters); microcomputer-based hardware/software designs; math-intensive algorithms; industrial controls; and avionics. The electronic test, assembly, and production area is well stocked with electronic test equipment. In addition, there is equipment for prototyping circuit boards, potting compounds, connector assembly equipment, automated electronic testing, programming EPROM chips, and programming one-time programmable microcomputer chips. The product prototyping area contains: 4-foot sheer, 4-foot bender, 3-foot finger bender, 6-inch corner cutter, Diacro punch, bandsaw, grinders, drill press, connector outline punch, and spot welder. Cyan is an Authorized Service Provider (ASP) for Hewlett-Packard. The list of equipment on which we perform authorized warranty service includes over 3,600 HP model numbers and covers a wide range of HP products. In conjunction with HP, we co-host a website for our customers to obtain HP-Guaranteed best prices on all HP products, supplies, and accessories.
