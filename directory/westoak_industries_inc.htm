  • History: WestOak Industries has been manufacturing printed circuit board assemblies since 1973. There's a reason for this incredible track record, and it starts with the relationships we've forged with our clients. The company stands on four profoundly important principles: integrity, service, quality, and reliability. Clients are supported in every aspect of the design and manufacturing process with our promise of quality and unconditional satisfaction guarantee. WestOak Industries is located in a modern 12,000 square foot facility. Our SMT assembly area is equipped with state of the art 3M conductive flooring. Production equipment includes lead formers, wire strippers, pick and place machines, forced convection and wave soldering machines. Engineering has a full compliment of CAD software for circuit design, PCB layout, mechanical and assembly drawing. On site is a complete machine shop for mechanical design. We have the flexibility to assist small business with limited volume and accommodate large businesses with higher volumes.
