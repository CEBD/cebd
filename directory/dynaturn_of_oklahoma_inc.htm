  • History: Dyna Turn is a production CNC machine shop specializing in close tolerance machining on all types of materials. The majority of our work consists of production and prototype machining as well as contract manufacturing. With more than 20,000 square feet of space, Dyna Turn's spacious factory is totally heated and air-conditioned and includes an inspection room of 1,200 square feet. Since 1983, Dyna Turn has specialized in precision machining, first as the manufacturer of precision computer disk substrate for hard disk drives. The quality standards required to manufacture those disks gave Dyna Turn the experience to become a quality producer in prototype and production CNC Manufacturing. Using stainless steel and other materials, Dyna Turn continually adds the most modern computerized equipment to our facilities to meet our customer demands.
  • Products: CNC Turning: Although the majority of Dyna Turn's parts are machined from stainless steel, we do have the capability of machining from various other raw materials ranging in size from small to medium. Detailed close tolerance work is commonplace in our facility. Surface finishes as low as .2 Ra have been produced on numerous parts. CNC Milling: Our machines are the most modern in CNC milling equipment, utilizing CAD/CAM programs. They are precise, rigid, and flexible affording us the capability to do quick set-up and change over. Other Equipment: With a complete conventional machine shop, we have machines capable of manual turning up to 42 inches in diameter and automatic sawing up to 12 inches.
  • Certifications: ISO 9002 compliant; registered with U.S. Department of Defense Central Contract Registry
