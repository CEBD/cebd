  • History: B Robison Enterprises was formed when the owner of Robison Solar Systems, Butch Robison, decided he would like to branch into building custom cars. After 14 years as Robison Solar, he sold the manufacturing of his DC water pumps to another Oklahoma based company and formed B Robison Enterprises. The new company, continues to sell, service, and tech the DC water pumps and solar systems. Butch has a vast knowledge of solar technology and is still researching and developing new products. The performance cars and vehicles have brought new excitement to the business. His love for performance vehicles is shown in his quality workmanship. His most recent completed project was a 1933 Ford and is currently in the final stages of completing a 1965 Shelby Cobra Replica. Some of his other vehicle projects have included 4WD off road, pro street, and performance race cars. The performance vehicles are built to the customer's specifications. Future plans are to add a line of Polished Stainless Fasteners to the company's line of products.
  • Products: Sales and Service of DC Water Pumps, DC Pump Controllers, Solar Modules, and Solar Water Pumping Systems. Sales and Service of Custom Performance Cars, Cobra Replicas, and 1933 Ford Replicas. Stainless Steel Polished Fastener sales.
