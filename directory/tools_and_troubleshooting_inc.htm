  • History: Established in 1975, Tools and Troubleshooting provides a variety of services. Started as a Mom and Pop shop, we have expanded from a 3,000 square foot space to over 13,000 square foot facility with six employees. Starting from a three machine basic operation, we have added Computer Numeric Controlled machines that have a working area that is four feet square. Ram and wire electrical discharge machining capabilities permit us to do virtually any machining operation. We have been using Computer Aided Design and Manufacturing since its inception. Our experience ranges from farm machinery design to jet engine nozzle design, from wire rope reel and drive design to dying nylon cord design and manufacturing, from injection, compression, and transfer mold design and manufacturing to a variety of trim and cutting die design and manufacturing.
  • Products: While primarily an injection mold design and manufacturing facility, we also design and manufacture a variety of other special equipment: Injection mold tooling, Compression mold tooling, Transfer mold tooling, Vacuum form tooling, Die cast tooling, Trim dies, PLC control integration, Air & Hydraulic systems, Processing equipment, Part and machine design, Part and machine manufacturing, Part and machine solutions, CAD/CAM services, CAD/CAM software, Consultation services, SLA models, Prototypes, CNC control retrofit
  • Certifications: Oklahoma Society of Professional Engineers; Texas Society of Professional Engineers; Oklahoma Section of the Society of Plastics Engineers; Oklahoma representative to the (International) Society of Plastics Engineers; Technical Speaker; Pilot
