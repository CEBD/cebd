OSBDC

The Oklahoma Small Business Development Center offers assistance in the fundamentals of starting and running a small business. Areas of assistance include development of business plans, marketing, legal structure, cash flow analysis, and human resource issues. The OSBDC also hosts workshops around the state that include topics such as business startup, taxation, financing, and programs for veterans and minorities.

Mission Statement

The mission of the Oklahoma Small Business Development Center is to provide help small businesses start, grow, and succeed.
