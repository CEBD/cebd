Industry Sectors of the Model

Not only is the economic modeling divided into 6 substate regions, but it is also divided into 70 industrial sectors. This means that the CEBD can modify industry-specific variables for economic impact scenarios that affect a limited number of industries. Furthermore, for every economic-impact scenario, the model will delineate the economic impacts upon each of the 70 sectors.

The CEBD can use this in-depth 70 sector REMI model to analyze the impacts that a particular industry would have upon Oklahoma's economy.
