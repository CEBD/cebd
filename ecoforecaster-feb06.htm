Economic Forecaster

The Economic Forecaster provides current and projected growth or decline of over 400 industries classified by North American Industry Classification System (NAICS.)  It gives the current and projected growth and decline of numerous occupations and provides the average earnings.  The Economic Forecaster also provides age, gender, and ethnicity of the population projected out to 2010.

Jobs by Industry

Jobs by Industry project the change in jobs for the different industries between the years 2005 and 2010, using the North American Industry Classification System (NAICS.)  Data columns include: NAICS code, base year jobs, projection year jobs, change in jobs, percentage change in jobs, and average annual earnings per worker.

Northwest Oklahoma Tulsa MSA

Jobs by Occupation

Jobs by Occupation project the change in existing occupations and the addition of new occupations in the region using the Standard Occupational Classification (SOC) System codes.  It also provides the average earnings and educational level of workers in each occupation.  The information is based on a beginning year of 2005 and a projection year of 2010.  The data columns include: occupation category, occupation code, base year jobs, projection year jobs, new jobs, replacement jobs, percentage of new and replacement jobs, and average hourly earnings per worker.

Northeast Oklahoma Oklahoma MSA
Northwest Oklahoma Tulsa MSA
Southeast Oklahoma SOIC Regions
Southwest Oklahoma Oklahoma

Demographics by Ethnicity

Demographics by Ethnicity make projections about the ethnicity of the population in the counties selected between the years 2005 and 2010.  Data columns include demographic category, base year population, percentage of population, projection year population, change in population, and percentage change in population.
