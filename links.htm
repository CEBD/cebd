New Business Development Newsletters Released

The Center for Economic & Business Development is proud to announce the latest installments in The Business Development Series:

Oklahoma Trade Pull Factors. This newsletter reports the trade pull factors of Oklahoma's seventy-seven counties and the trade pull factors of fifty Oklahoma communities. Trade pull factors relate a county's or city's ability to attract both resident and non-resident retail shoppers to its community through retail trade.

Average Business | County. This newsletter reports the average number of businesses per county competing in a given industry for counties of similar population size in Oklahoma. This comparison allows business professionals to identify potential opportunities or threats in their local economy.

Threshold Analysis. This newsletter reports the average number of people it takes to support a business. Threshold figures are benchmarks that can be used for comparative purposes. One can use these benchmark figures to discover potential opportunities for new business start-ups and/or expansion in the local economy.
