Economic Forecaster April - July 2007

The Economic Forecaster provides current and projected growth and decline of over 400 industries classified by the North American Industry Classification System (NAICS).  It provides current statistics and ten-year projections for growth and decline of jobs by industry, jobs by occupation, and demographics for a selected region, in addition to current unemployment data.

Industry Tools Occupation Tools
Demographic Tools Economy Overview Tools

Jobs by Industry

The Jobs by Industry Report allows users to view an expandable table of all regional, state, and national industry data.  Data columns include: NAICS code, base year jobs, projection year jobs, change in jobs, percent change in jobs, average annual earnings per worker, national percent change in jobs, national average annual earnings per worker, and data level.
