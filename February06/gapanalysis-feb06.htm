Economic Impact

The Economic Impact provides a quantifiable and economically sound method for determining the relative impact of proposed or impending changes to a regional economy. It allows users to discover which sectors of the local region actually drive the economy.

Gap Analysis

Gap Analysis displays in dollars how much an industry purchases from other industries, and the percent of those purchases that are satisfied from inside and outside of the region. This shows how dependent an industry is on imports.
