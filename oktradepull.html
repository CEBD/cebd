Oklahoma Trade Pull Factors

The Trade Area Capture and Trade Pull Factors for the Retail Trade Sectors newsletter reports the trade area capture, which is the estimate of the number of customers who have shopped in the local economy over a one-year period. It also provides the trade pull factors, which measures a region's ability to attract shoppers to make retail purchases within the region.
