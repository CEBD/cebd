Alliance Membership

What does Alliance membership mean to your company? Alliance partners receive a wide range of benefits to help them compete in today's fast-moving global marketplace.

Membership Benefits

  • One-on-one consultation with a Manufacturing Extention Agent (MEA)
  • Resources for ISO 9000 and ISO 14000 consulting
  • Resources to Lean Manufacturing principles
  • Product commercialization assistance
  • Performance assessment at no charge
  • Organizational development through a manufacturing council
  • Access to a network of service providers
  • Assistance in accessing international markets
  • Access to Applications Engineers
